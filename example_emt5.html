MedDRA CT Mapping - ICD-10 and SNOMED CT

The Medical Dictionary for Regulatory Activities (MedDRA) is a set of standardized terminologies commonly used in clinical safety recording, communicating, and reporting activities. MedDRA is developed and maintained by the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH)[1],[2].

There are also collaborates between MedDRA and other CTs. Mapping files are available to be downloaded on MedDRA website after login[3],[4].

The module emt currently does not include functions for working with other CTs. This is a place holder for future uses.

Reference

[1]

ICH. (2013). Understanding MedDRA. (pdf)

[2]

Dutta, A. (2020). MedDRA - Terminologies & Coding. (pdf)

[3]

[4]
