MedDRA - work with SMQ

The Medical Dictionary for Regulatory Activities (MedDRA) is a set of standardized terminologies commonly used in clinical safety recording, communicating, and reporting activities. MedDRA is developed and maintained by the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH)[1],[2]. The Standardised MedDRA Queries (SMQs) are groups of MedDRA terms used to describe conditions of interest. The module emt also includes basic functions for working with MedDRA SMQ.

Assign MedDRA Distribution Folder

Please see more details here.

from mtbp3.util.emt import Emt

emt = Emt(folder_name='')
print(emt.find_files())

Find SMQ Terms

To find a list of SMQ:

smq_names = emt.find_smq()

To find more details about a SMQ:

print(emt.find_smq(smq_names[0]).T)
